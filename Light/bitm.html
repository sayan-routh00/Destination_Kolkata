Birla Industrial & Technological Museum (BITM)

BITM is one of India’s earliest science museums focused on industrial and technological education with hands-on exhibits, demonstration models and regular workshops for students.

Exhibits & Education

Run with educational outreach in mind, BITM hosts displays on electricity, mechanics, communications, materials and industrial processes. It is a popular destination for school visits and science workshops.

Visitor practicals

  • Opening hours: Usually morning to early evening for galleries; some days closed for maintenance or government holidays — check ahead.
  • Suggested visit duration: 1–2 hours.
  • Accessibility: Facilities vary by gallery; contact BITM for special assistance and school group bookings.

Tickets & Booking

  • Fees: Modest entry fees typically apply; discounts for students and groups are common — confirm current rates at the gate or via official channels.
  • Online booking: BITM may support group or workshop bookings via official museum contacts — check their website or contact the staff directly.
  • Official site: bitm.org.in

Allowed / Restricted items

  • Cameras for personal use are usually allowed; some demonstration areas may prohibit photography.
  • Food is typically restricted to designated areas; school groups should follow staff instructions.

Contact & Location

  • Address: 19A Gurusaday Road, Kolkata – 700019, West Bengal, India
  • Coordinates: 22.5344° N, 88.3636° E
  • Phone: 9477345291, 9477345292
  • Email: director@bitm.gov.in

Upcoming Events

BITM runs workshops, demonstration sessions and school outreach programmes — check with museum staff for the schedule.

Visitor tips

  • Great for school groups — contact ahead for workshop bookings.
  • Carry student ID for concessions if applicable.
