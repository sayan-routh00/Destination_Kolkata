Science City

Science City is a major interactive science centre in Kolkata, offering hands-on exhibits, a Space Theatre, 3D shows and demonstrations designed for families, students and science enthusiasts.

What to expect

Science City features multiple galleries with interactive exhibits on physics, energy, environment and technology, along with an IMAX / Space Theatre and live science demonstrations. It is a high-energy destination for children and adults who enjoy experiential learning.

Visitor practicals

  • Opening hours: Typically morning to early evening for galleries and theatre show schedules; theatre shows have set times — confirm timings on the official site before visiting.
  • Suggested visit duration: 2–4 hours depending on shows and exhibits you choose to see.
  • Accessibility: Most public areas are accessible; check in advance for any show-specific restrictions.

Tickets & Booking

  • Ticket types: Separate tickets may be required for Space Theatre / IMAX shows and special exhibitions — prices vary by category (adult/child/foreign).
  • Online booking: Science City offers online ticketing for some shows and events — check the official Science City ticketing portal or contact their help desk.
  • Official site: sciencecitykolkata.org.in

Allowed / Restricted items

  • Cameras and phones for personal use are typically allowed; follow signage in theatres (no flash, and mobile devices may need to be switched off during shows).
  • Food and beverages may be limited to designated areas; large bags may be checked at entry.

Contact & Location

  • Coordinates: 22.5405° N, 88.3958° E
  • Phone: (033) 2285 4343

Upcoming Events

Science City hosts temporary exhibitions, camps and special showtimes. Check the official calendar for the latest events.

Visitor tips

  • Pre-book shows if available (Space Theatre / 3D/IMAX) to secure seats at popular times.
  • Arrive early for demonstrations and allow time for queues at interactive exhibits on weekends and holidays.
